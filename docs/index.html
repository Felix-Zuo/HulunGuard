HulunGuard watches the work trail: claims, evidence, tools, failures, checkpoints, and final gates.

Open repository See the runtime

Truth lives in the trail.

A fluent answer is not enough. HulunGuard keeps the execution state outside the chat and checks whether the work is still grounded.

Claims need backing

Completion and verification claims are weighed against tests, files, trace references, commits, and approvals.

Failures stay visible

Broken tools, failed checks, unresolved loops, and skipped recoveries stay in the ledger until they are handled.

Conversations have pulse

Live sessions track user challenges, pending tools, summary drift, and final attempts as runtime events.

A meter for the moment before final.

Green means continue. Yellow means calibrate. Red means recover before any final claim leaves the room.
